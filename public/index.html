on-device analysis

Your face is graded in your browser. Landmarks never leave the device. We compute symmetry, harmonic ratios, jawline definition, canthal tilt, and skin uniformity — then collapse it into a PSL score.

ranked matchmaking

Few people online? You can fight anyone. Past 50 active players, the queue pairs you with someone in your ELO band so your climb means something. Tier badges from bronze to state-mogger.

frequently asked

does anything leave my device?

No video frames or landmarks are uploaded. Only the final composite PSL number is sent to score the round. Your PFP, if you upload one, is stored on the server so the leaderboard can show it.

what is psl?

A community shorthand for a holistic facial-aesthetic score (0–10). V2 derives it from five measurable axes; treat it as a game metric, not a verdict on you.

can i change my handle?

Yes — go through the claim card again. As long as the new name is free and clean, it's yours.

do friend matches count?

No. Private rooms are unranked — for the clip, not the climb.
