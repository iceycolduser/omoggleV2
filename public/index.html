frequently asked

does anything leave my device?

No video frames or landmarks are uploaded. Only the final composite PSL number is sent to score the round.

what is psl?

A community shorthand for a holistic facial-aesthetic score (0–10). V2 derives it from five measurable axes; treat it as a game metric, not a verdict on you.

can i skip?

Yes — skip, mute, and report are one tap each. Skips early in the round are free; conceding mid-round costs ELO.

is this just omoggle.com?

Inspired by it. V2 has a five-axis breakdown, ELO-band queue, live overlay, mobile layout, and runs on our own infra so we can keep it sharp.
