privacy

what we store

  • A randomly-generated player id and your chosen handle, stored in your browser and on our server.
  • Your ELO, win/loss/draw/mog counts, and your most recent composite PSL score.
  • Match metadata (id, participants, composite PSL on each side, winner, timestamps).

what we do not store

  • No video frames are uploaded or recorded by the service.
  • No facial landmarks, embeddings, or images leave your device.
  • No account, email, or social signup is required.

peer connection

Live video is sent peer-to-peer via WebRTC. Your IP address is necessarily visible to the peer you are matched with (this is how WebRTC works). A TURN relay can be configured to mask it.

data deletion

Clearing browser storage detaches you from your player id. To erase server-side stats, contact the operator with your player id.
